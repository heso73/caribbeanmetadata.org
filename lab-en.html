The engine behind the CMS

Caribwood Language Lab

The intellectual and technical infrastructure that develops, maintains, and deploys the Caribbean Metadata Standard.

Mission

Building a digital infrastructure for Caribbean culture

The Caribwood Language Lab is the research and development laboratory of the Caribwood ecosystem. Its mission is to build the tools, standards, and protocols that allow Caribbean content to exist fully in the global digital economy.

It operates at the intersection of linguistics, technology, culture, and artificial intelligence — with a strong territorial grounding in Guadeloupe and the wider Caribbean.

"We don't just produce content. We build the vocabulary that allows the world to understand it."
VISION

Make the Caribbean a global reference for digital cultural sovereignty.

GROUNDING

Guadeloupe, Caribbean — with a regional and international vocation.

APPROACH

Field first. Every tool is born from observed reality, not imported theory.
