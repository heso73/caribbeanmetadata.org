Press releases

Mai 2026 Official launch

Launch of the Caribbean Metadata Standard v2.0 — the first Caribbean digital standard

The Caribwood Language Lab announces the official publication of the Caribbean Metadata Standard v2.0, an unprecedented framework for classifying, describing and indexing Caribbean audiovisual content. This standard aims to end the algorithmic invisibility of creators from the region.

Read full press release

"The Caribbean is not a fragment of the world: it is a whole world."

Le CMS est notre réponse structurée à des décennies d'invisibilité numérique — un alphabet pour que les algorithmes comprennent enfin nos cultures.

Avril 2026 Nouveau

Publication of the first Caribbean open data corpus on GitHub

The CMS publishes under CC-BY 4.0 the first structured corpus of Caribbean cultural data — 15 records, 8 languages, 15 cultural markers.

Submission of CMS as regional standard to CROSQ

The Caribbean Metadata Standard was officially submitted to the Caribbean Regional Organisation for Standards and Quality (CROSQ) under the NWIP procedure.
